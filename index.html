Sales Forecasting for slow moving SKUs

To predict the 7-day sales forecast for slow-moving items, an ensemble framework was constructed. This ensemble framework combined the power of three distinct models: Facebook Prophet, MSTL decomposition, and Hidden Markov models. The integration of these models led to the achievement of an impressively low Mean Squared Error (MSE) of 2.21. Importantly, this MSE was 58.23% better than that of the Null model, demonstrating the effectiveness of the ensemble approach in improving sales forecast accuracy and highlighting its potential for enhancing decision-making in inventory management and sales planning.
